Description

  • Luxurious, weather-resistant 0.75" wide rattan wicker
  • Durable, heavy-duty powder coated aluminum frame is rust proof
  • Unique multi-toned rattan rope detailing on armrests and legs
  • Textured UV resistant Olefin fabric seat and backrest cushion covers
  • Beautifully accented rattan-wrapped legs
  • Ergonomic leg design optimizes comfort
  • No assembly required

Bring your outdoor seating area to life with this stunning wide-weave outdoor patio chair - a beautiful design paired with comfort and durability. Featuring thick foam cushions covered in UV resistant Olefin fabric, this patio chair is constructed of a solid aluminum frame that is truly built to last. Meticulously wrapped in a wide, UV and weather-resistant rattan and accented with gorgeous multi-toned rattan rope detailing, this patio chair is the perfect addition to your outdoor entertaining area.

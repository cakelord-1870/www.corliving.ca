Description

  • Umbrella made of durable, UV resistant polyester fabric
  • Heavy duty dark grey aluminum frame and steel base with 8 metal support arms
  • 11.5ft wide umbrella canopy for optimal coverage
  • Convenient crank open system for easy open and collapse
  • Easy-glide manual lever controls adjustable tilting angles for optimal shade
  • Wide range tilt options lock into place for added stability
  • 360 degree rotation using an easy access foot pedal
  • Vented canopy for added air circulation
  • Secure T base with umbrella base weights for additional support (Base sold separately) 

Sharing special moments in your own backyard is easy with this 11.5ft deluxe offset patio umbrella This versatile offset umbrella can be used to create shade for a table or seating area making a comfortable shady spot on your patio. Made with durable aluminum and steel frame and UV resistant umbrella fabric this product looks great and is ready to withstand the elements of the outdoors. With easy-glide lever to control your tilt angles, create some shade to keep cool and enjoy your outdoor seating area.

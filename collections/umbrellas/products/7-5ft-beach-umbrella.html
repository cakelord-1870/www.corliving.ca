Description

  • Umbrella made of durable, UV resistant polyester fabric
  • Heavy duty dark grey steel frame with 6 metal support arms
  • 7.5ft wide umbrella canopy for optimal coverage
  • Umbrella stands 8ft tall (without auger attachment) and has a pole diameter of 1.25"
  • Vented canopy and 3 wind tension setting provides stability during windy days
  • Auger attachment firmly anchors umbrella in sand or dirt
  • Can be used with both a patio table or umbrella base
  • Includes convenient carry bag for transportation
  • Pair with umbrella base for additional support on your patio (Base sold separately)

From your patio to the beach, this umbrella is designed to keep you cool, while protecting you from the sun with its UV resistant fabric. This beach umbrella doubles as a patio umbrella for added versatility and can be paired with a CorLiving umbrella base for additional support on your patio. The vented canopy and wind tension settings provide added stability during windy days. Included is a carry bag that makes it easy to transport for any outdoor occasion.
